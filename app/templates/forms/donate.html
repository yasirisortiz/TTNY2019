GreenStand exists to create a tool that can make our earth a greener, healthier place. To do this on a grand scale, it must also provide solutions to the dire needs of our fellow humans. We look to provide an alternative solution for the millions who are struggling as they make less than a dollar a day turning rainforest into charcoal. With your help, we believe we can find solutions.

GreenStand is a registered 501C-3 non-profit, and your gift will be fully tax deductible. We are a volunteer team and proud to put all our funds directly into the development of this project.

For more details of how we use our funds please email us Info@greenstand.org
